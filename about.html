ABOUT US

Founded in 1985, Quma Learning has had the privilege of working with and for many of the finest organizations in the world today.

Through its highly lauded human development training Quma Learning has forged an impressive record for building stronger people and organizations from the inside out.

DR. DENNIS DEATON

Dennis R Deaton is the co-founder and CEO of Quma Learning Systems, an award-winning author, and the chief designer of Quma's most sought-after seminars, including the Ownership Spirit®, Visioneering, and Destination Thinking for Leaders®.

Dr. Deaton is a popular speaker at conferences, international meetings, and conventions, and author of five books, including the award-winning Ownership Spirit: The One Grand Key That Changes Everything Else.
