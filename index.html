DESTINATION THINKING FOR LEADERS

The impact of a shared team vision cannot be overestimated. It infuses teams with energy, creativity, and commitment. It ignites a pervasive "we can and we will" mindset in every role and every player.

- Hide -

Can every member of your organization truthfully say yes to the following?

  • We know where we are going!
  • We know what it looks like when we get there!
  • We know why it matters!

Before tactics, there must be strategy; before strategy, there must be vision!

  • Align and focus your team on the identical destination
  • Avoid mixed messaging that undermines team unity and confidence
  • Define a clear sense of higher purpose
  • Inspire team members to create their own vision within the vision
